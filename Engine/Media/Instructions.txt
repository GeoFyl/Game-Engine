Controls:
- Right-click to start/pause simulation
- WASD to roll ball
- Q and E to rotate
- Space to jump
- F to toggle wireframe mode
- L to toggle lighting
- C and V to switch camera position
- 1 and 2 to switch between scenes
- Enter to play a sound (easier to hear it in scene 2)
- Click on this text, cursor should warp to centre of screen"
